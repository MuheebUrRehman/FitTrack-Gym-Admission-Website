Body Mass Index

What is BMI?

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a tool used to gauge if someone's weight is healthy for their height. It's a simple calculation that gives you an idea of whether you're underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. Knowing your BMI can help you and your doctor keep an eye on your overall health and make informed decisions about your lifestyle, like diet and exercise
